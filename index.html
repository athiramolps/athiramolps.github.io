1. Power BI — Sales Dashboard Power BI • DAX • Data Modeling

Business Problem

  • LARA Company needed a data-driven insight into sales performance, growth patterns, and revenue trends.
  • The business lacked a centralized view of its sales operations, making it difficult to identify top-performing products, high-value customers, or key revenue drivers.

Goals

  • Conduct sales analysis across all categories and products.
  • Track KPI metrics such as revenue, profit, and growth rates.
  • Analyze category-level sales to identify top-performing segments.
  • Examine yearly sales and profit trends to monitor growth.
  • Identify top cities and customers by sales for targeted strategies.
  • Evaluate sales composition by product hierarchy for detailed insights.
  • Provide strategy recommendations for revenue growth and performance improvement.

Analysis Steps

  • Collected raw sales data from multiple sources.
  • Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query.
  • Designed star schema data model with relationships between fact and dimension tables.
  • Created DAX measures for KPIs (Revenue, Profit, YoY Growth, etc.).
  • Built visualizations (bar charts, line charts, pie charts, maps) for analysis.
  • Applied filters, slicers, and drill-throughs for interactivity.
  • Developed a user-friendly dashboard layout with consistent design and navigation.

What Output Got?

  • An interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard featuring:
  • KPIs showing overall revenue, profit, and growth performance.
  • Category and product-level analysis for detailed sales tracking.
  • Year-over-year sales and profit trends visualizations.
  • Insights on top-performing cities and customers.
  • Sales composition charts by product hierarchy.
  • Evidence-based strategic recommendations for management decisions.
  • A centralized reporting tool enabling data-driven decision-making and performance optimization.

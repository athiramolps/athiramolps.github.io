3. Tableau — Admissions Dashboard | Education Tableau • Education • Data Visualization

Business Problem

  • Educational institutions often struggle to manage and interpret large volumes of applicant data.
  • Without a centralized, visual reporting system, it is difficult to identify trends and monitor applicant conversion.

Goals

  • Develop an interactive admissions dashboard to visualize applications and enrollments.
  • Provide insights into program-level demand, especially for medical-related programs.
  • Segment applicants by demographics (age, gender, ethnicity, country) to support diversity tracking.
  • Enable drill-down into individual applicant details for deeper analysis.
  • Support decision-making for admissions officers and academic planners.

What Output Got?

  • A Tableau Admissions Dashboard with multiple views: Overview, Programs, Demographics, Applicant Details.
  • Improved ability for stakeholders to monitor admissions performance and analyze applicant diversity.
  • A scalable solution that supports data-driven admissions planning and reporting.

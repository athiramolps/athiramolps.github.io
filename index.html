10. Tableau — Camden Traffic Accidents Analysis (2015–2019) Tableau • Traffic Analysis • Data Visualization

Business Problem

  • Camden, UK faced frequent road traffic accidents, but limited insight into causes, severity, timing, and road user involvement.
  • This made it challenging for authorities to implement effective road safety measures.

Goals

  • Analyze accident severity (fatal, serious, slight) to understand collision impact.
  • Examine temporal patterns to identify peak accident times and days.
  • Identify types of road users involved (pedestrians, cyclists, drivers).
  • Map accident hotspots to support targeted safety interventions.
  • Explore contributing factors like weather, lighting, and road conditions.

What Output Got?

  • Interactive Tableau dashboard showing accident severity, trends, and road user involvement.
  • Geographical hotspot mapping highlighting high-risk locations in Camden.
  • Insights into contributing factors and patterns for planners and authorities.
  • A comprehensive visualization tool for monitoring, analyzing, and planning interventions.
